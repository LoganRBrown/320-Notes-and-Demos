Game Demo Protocol

Packets from client
===================

JOIN - Sent when joinging the game, this packet should "register the player".

	0	L	Datatype	Desc
	===============================
	0	4	ascii		"JOIN"

Packets from server
===================

BALL - The balls's position. sent 60 times per second

	O	L	Datatype	Desc
	===============================
	0	4	ascii		"JOIN"
	4	4	float		x position
	8	4	float		y position
	12	4	float		z position
